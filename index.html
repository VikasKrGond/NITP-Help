Some Most Frequently Asked Questions

Initially you should learn C language as it is considered as mother of all languages it clears our basic concepts about programming languages.After that you should move towards python as it is a trending language very simple to learn with huge application and demand in the IT sector. Don't use Python, Ruby, etc, for CP. These are very high-level languages that won't give you as much control over your code as needed.
First of All Competetive is not necessary for Placement but It does help you in Coding Round as you already solved tonnes of problems. To start you first move to Hackerrank and Solve Question for C/C++ or Java and spend at least one month afterward you can move to Codechef(in beginning participate in Long Challenge and Starters) and when you are three Star or more in Codechef then you can Move to the tougher platform Codeforces and start giving as many competitions as you can but try to solve the unsolved question after every competition.
Talking about CGPA it is preferable to have a CGPA above 8 for your smooth placement and to be very honest it's too easy to get a CGPA above 8 just listen to the class and revise all this a week before the exam and you are done. Regarding skill, it is a must to have industry-relevant skills on your portfolio to land up your dream job, and to achieve this most precious tool is youtube where you can get everything for free. So what are you waiting for start now.
DSA(Data Structure and Algorithm) is the most important subject for your placement . So to start with it first clear basics of atlest one programming language(prefrably C/C++/Java) then you can some of the youtube tutorials like: Code with harry or Apni Kaksha or any other of your choice. After covering basics of every Data Structure try to solve quetion in ratio of 30% Easy 50% Medium and 20% Difficult for in depth Understanding from GFG or Leetcode
Web Delopment is the most easy to start domain for Development.To start with web dev first you need to know HTML and CSS to make simple static Website . After getting experiene in HTML and CSS you have to Learn Javascript to make dynamic website and finally after getting some experience in Javascript also you have to learn frameworks to build more complex site and for Backend.
Hackethons are the time bound event to test your problem solving ability. It is always good to participate in as much hackethons you can particate because from each hackethon you are making a real life problem solving project and that of pressure of deadline which is very much similar to your job. In hackethos you get a chance to meet like mindedd people and connect with them eventually.
Internships are the most closer to your real MNC job which you'll be getting in placement it is always a good plan to do atleast one internship in your college era it'll give you real world taste of work and tonnes of new skill and networking morevoer you can get a PPO(Pre Placement Offer) from where you are interning. The most aducate time to do internship is in your 3rd year .
The most importan one are Kick Start , Code Jam , Facebook Hacker Cup , Hash Code GSOC,MLH Fellowship,ICPC
